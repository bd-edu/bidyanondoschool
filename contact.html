Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How can I verify Global Hope Initiative's legitimacy?

We are a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization in the United States (EIN: 12-3456789). Our financial statements are audited annually and available on our website. We hold a 4-star rating from Charity Navigator and the Platinum Seal of Transparency from Guidestar.

What percentage of my donation goes directly to programs?

87% of every dollar donated goes directly to our programs in the field. 8% covers administrative costs, and 5% is allocated to fundraising activities. We maintain this efficiency through careful budgeting and leveraging partnerships with local organizations.

Can I donate to a specific country or program?

Yes, you can designate your donation to a specific program area (education, healthcare, clean water, or women's empowerment) or region. However, we encourage unrestricted donations as they allow us to allocate resources where they're most needed and respond to emergencies.

How do I update my monthly donation or contact information?

Monthly donors can update their payment information, donation amount, or contact details by emailing donorservices@globalhope.org or calling our donor services line at +1 (202) 555-0199. You can also log into your donor portal on our website.

Do you accept in-kind donations?

We accept certain in-kind donations such as medical supplies, educational materials, and technology equipment. Please contact inkind@globalhope.org with details about your proposed donation before shipping any items to ensure we can utilize them effectively.
