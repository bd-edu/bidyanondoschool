Regional Focus Areas

Tailored programs addressing specific regional challenges

Sub-Saharan Africa

Focus Countries: Kenya, Malawi, Senegal, Ethiopia, Democratic Republic of Congo

Primary Programs: Clean water, maternal healthcare, agricultural development, girls' education

Impact: Provided clean water access to 500,000+ people, built 80 schools, trained 2,000 healthcare workers

South & Southeast Asia

Focus Countries: Bangladesh, Nepal, Philippines, Indonesia, Myanmar

Primary Programs: Disaster resilience, child protection, vocational training, climate adaptation

Impact: Established 30 disaster-resilient communities, provided scholarships to 10,000 students, trained 5,000 farmers in sustainable agriculture

Latin America & Caribbean

Focus Countries: Haiti, Guatemala, Honduras, Colombia, Peru

Primary Programs: Youth empowerment, indigenous rights, food security, violence prevention

Impact: Created 150 youth-led enterprises, established 40 community gardens, reduced youth violence by 60% in target communities

Middle East & North Africa

Focus Countries: Jordan, Lebanon, Yemen, Iraq, Sudan

Primary Programs: Refugee support, trauma counseling, education in emergencies, livelihood restoration

Impact: Provided education to 20,000 refugee children, offered psychological support to 15,000+ individuals, created 5,000 livelihood opportunities
